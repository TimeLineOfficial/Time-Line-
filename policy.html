General Policy

Welcome to TIME LINE, your trusted delivery service for special moments. Our general policies are designed to ensure a smooth and satisfactory experience for all our customers.

Service Areas: We currently operate in Varanasi and Chandauli. We're continuously expanding our service areas to reach more locations.

Operating Hours: Our standard delivery hours are from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week. Special arrangements can be made for deliveries outside these hours with additional charges.

Booking Process: Orders should be placed at least 3 hours in advance for same-day deliveries. For special occasions like Valentine's Day, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance.

Customer Responsibilities:

  • Provide accurate delivery information including address, contact details, and delivery instructions
  • Ensure the recipient is available to receive the delivery
  • Inform us of any special handling requirements for items
  • Notify us of any changes to the order at least 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time

Service Limitations: We do not deliver prohibited or illegal items. We reserve the right to refuse delivery of items that may pose safety risks or violate local regulations.

Communication: We will keep you updated about your delivery status through WhatsApp or SMS. You can also contact our customer service for any queries or concerns.

Delivery Policy

At TIME LINE, we strive to deliver your items safely and on time. Our delivery policy outlines the procedures and expectations for our delivery services.

Delivery Timeframes:

  • Standard Delivery: Items will be delivered within the specified time slot selected during order placement
  • Express Delivery: Available for urgent deliveries with additional charges
  • Scheduled Delivery: You can schedule deliveries up to 30 days in advance

Delivery Confirmation: Our delivery agent will confirm the delivery through photos or videos as proof of delivery. For live deliveries, you can watch the delivery in real-time through your selected platform.

Failed Deliveries: If the recipient is not available at the time of delivery, our delivery agent will:

  • Call the recipient to coordinate an alternative delivery time
  • Wait for up to 15 minutes at the delivery location
  • If delivery still cannot be completed, the item will be returned to our facility
  • A second delivery attempt will be scheduled at an additional charge

Item Handling: We take utmost care in handling your items during delivery. However, we recommend not sending extremely fragile items or items of high monetary value without proper packaging.

Live/Recorded Delivery: For customers who opt for live or recorded delivery:

  • Live delivery allows you to watch the delivery in real-time through WhatsApp, Google Meet, or Instagram
  • Recorded delivery provides a video recording of the delivery which will be shared with you after completion
  • Technical issues may occasionally affect live streaming quality

Delivery Restrictions: We reserve the right to refuse delivery in areas that are deemed unsafe or inaccessible. In such cases, we will coordinate with you for an alternative delivery location.

Payment & Refund Policy

Our payment and refund policies are designed to provide transparency and fairness in all financial transactions.

Payment Methods: We accept payments through:

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Cash on Delivery (for select orders)

Pricing Structure:

  • Base delivery charge: ₹249 for standard deliveries
  • Item purchase service: ₹299 plus the cost of items
  • Live/Recorded delivery: Additional ₹50
  • Distance charges may apply for locations beyond 5km from our service center
  • Special occasion surcharges may apply on Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, etc.

Refund Policy:

  • Full refund (excluding distance charges) if delivery fails due to our fault
  • Refunds will be processed within 12 hours of the failed delivery
  • No refund for cancellations made less than 3 hours before the scheduled delivery time
  • 50% refund for cancellations made 3-12 hours before the scheduled delivery time
  • Full refund for cancellations made more than 12 hours before the scheduled delivery time

Refund Process: Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the order. In case of any issues with the refund, please contact our customer service.

Additional Charges: Any additional services requested after order placement will be charged separately and must be paid before the service is provided.

Privacy Policy

At TIME LINE, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect:

  • Contact information (name, phone number, email address)
  • Delivery addresses
  • Payment information
  • Order details and preferences
  • Feedback and communication records

How We Use Your Information:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your deliveries
  • To improve our services and customer experience
  • To send promotional offers and updates (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Delivery partners who help us complete your orders
  • Payment processors to facilitate transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights: You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Cookies and Tracking: Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit some functionality.

Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at timelinedeliveryofficial@gmail.com.
